## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I log a game result?**  
After finishing a puzzle, tap the Share button inside the game (Wordle, Connections, etc.) and choose StreakSync from the share sheet. The result is parsed and your streak updates automatically.

**My streak shows as broken but I played every day — what happened?**  
Go to Settings → Data Management and tap "Rebuild Streaks". If a result is missing, use the share sheet from the game again for that day.

**How do I add friends?**  
Sign in with Apple (Settings → Account), then go to the Friends tab and tap the person+ icon. Share your 6-character friend code or enter a friend's code.

**How is my data stored?**  
Game results and streaks are stored on your device and synced to your own Firebase account. We never share your data. See the [Privacy Policy](https://mit112.github.io/StreakSync/privacy).

**How do I delete my account and all data?**  
Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes all data from our servers.
